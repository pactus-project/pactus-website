Focused on decentralization

Pactus achieves true decentralization by removing delegation and mining while fostering an open, inclusive network that empowers direct user participation.

User-friendly Blockchain

Pactus provides an intuitive, user-friendly blockchain experience with a streamlined GUI, enabling anyone to easily run nodes and participate in the network without technical barriers.

Sandbox dApps Platform

Pactus revolutionizes decentralized app development with its Sandbox dApps platform, offering secure storage, seamless contract deployment, and a flexible environment for innovation.

Solid State Proof of Stake

Pactus' SSPoS consensus, verified with TLA+, ensures security and decentralization through dynamic, fault-tolerant validator committees, eliminating delegation.

Gas-less Transactions

Pactus consumptional fee model allows users to make up to two zero-fee transactions daily, reducing costs and driving blockchain adoption.

Near-Zero Energy Staking

Pactus enables sustainable blockchain participation with its lightweight node, running seamlessly on Raspberry Pi Zero. Achieving near-zero energy consumption at just 0.7W power.

Get started

To start your Pactus journey, you will need to download the latest software. Pactus is designed to be lightweight and user-friendly, offering support for all operating systems and infrastructures with our GUI and CLI applications.

02
Obtain PAC

Before you can utilize all that Pactus has to offer, you will need to obtain our native coin. PAC is a currency that can be exchanged for products, services and other currencies with a percentage fee based model of 0.01% per tx.

Become a validator

You can become a Pactus validator by staking some coins to your node. As a Pactus validator you will earn rewards, these are given for collecting valid transactions and creating new blocks. The emission is one coin per block.
