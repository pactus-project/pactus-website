We are excited to announce that **validator delegation is now live on the Pactus network**,
following the activation of [PIP-49](https://pips.pactus.org/PIPs/pip-49),
in latest version, [Pactus Tallinn](https://pactus.org/2026/04/04/pactus-1.13.0-tallinn-released/).

There has been strong interest from validators across the ecosystem,
especially from those who are not yet part of Pactus but are eager to participate.

At the same time, we recognize that current market conditions and
uncertainty can make it difficult to acquire PAC tokens and join the network.

To address this challenge and lower the barrier to entry, we have introduced validator delegation.

## What Is Validator Delegation?

Validator delegation allows PAC holders to delegate their tokens to validator operators.
This enables validators to participate in block production and earn rewards
**without needing to purchase tokens upfront**.

This mechanism benefits both token holders and validators:

* Token holders can earn rewards by delegating their stake
* Validators can join and operate without initial capital requirements

## Special Opportunity for New Validators

As part of this rollout, we are offering a limited opportunity specifically for validators
who are not yet part of the Pactus network, including those already operating on other blockchain networks.

Selected participants will receive:

* **16,000 PAC in delegated stake**
* Distributed across **16 validators (1,000 PAC each)**
* **No cost** to receive this delegation

## Rewards Structure

Validators participating through this program will:

* Earn **50% of the block rewards (0.5 PAC per block)**
* Receive all rewards directly to their own wallet
* Maintain full control over their reward addresses

## Lightweight Infrastructure Requirements

Running a Pactus validator is intentionally simple and efficient:

* Requires only **1–2 CPU cores**
* Can run on a **shared server**
* No dedicated hardware or complex infrastructure needed

This makes it easy for experienced validators to integrate Pactus into their existing operations.

## Why We’re Doing This

Our goal is to:

* Expand validator participation
* Improve decentralization
* Distribute stake more broadly across the network

By enabling more validators—especially new entrants—to join,
we strengthen the long-term resilience and value of the Pactus ecosystem.

## Getting Started

Joining the network is straightforward:

1. Setup and [start](https://docs.pactus.org/get-started/pactus-daemon/) your Pactus validator.

2. Share the addresses of your first 16 validators with us.

3. Our team will delegate the stake to activate them.

This is a unique opportunity to become part of a growing and
innovative blockchain network at an early stage—without the usual barriers to entry.

If you are already validating on other networks, now is the perfect time to expand into Pactus.
